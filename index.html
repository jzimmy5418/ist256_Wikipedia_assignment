Joshua Robert Zimmerman was born on May 10th 2005 and resides in Newmanstown, Pennsylvania. He is currently studying Information Sciences and Technology at The Pennsylvania State University. Prior to college Josh graduated from Eastern Lebanon County High School in Myerstown Pennsylvania.

Early Life

Josh was born on May 10th 2005 in the Wellspan Good Samaritan Hospital in Lebanon, Pennsylvania. He is the son of Jason M. Zimmerman, who worked as a project manager for Goodman Telecom (now defunct), and Christine A. Saul, whom worked as an office secretary in the Lebanon County district attorney's office. During his early life he lived along Memorial Boulevard in Newmanstown before moving to his current place of residence in Newburg Village. His sister, Ashley, was born in October 2008. He spent much of his early life, before elementary school & during summers, at one of his grandparents' houses. He went to church at Myerstown Church of the Brethren before moving to the Birdge of Hope Church in Womelsdorf PA.

School Career

Josh started his schooling by going to preschool at Teach-a-Tot preschool in Richland PA at the age of 4 years. He began elementary school at Fort Zeller elementary school in Richland for grades K-2, followed by grades 3-5 at the Eastern Lebanon County School District (ELCO) Intermediate School in Myerstown PA. Following this he attended the ELCO Middle School also in Myerstown, where he was a member of the track and field team for one season as well as NJHS. He attended ELCO High School as well and was a member of the physics & RaiderThon clubs. He graduated in 2023, 3rd in his class with a 3.97 GPA. Following graduation from ELCO High he began college at The Pennsylvania State University's Berks campus in teh fall of 2023 studying for an associates degree in information sciences and technology.

Teenage Life

Throughout his teenage years josh found hobbies such as gaming, railroad photography, model railroading, coding, fishing, cycling, & kayaking. He received his driver's licence in 2021 and began his job at Dutchway Farm Market earlier that same year. He began his career as a cashier before being promoted to a checkout charge person in 2022 followed closely behind by a promotion to a front end office person. He also acted as a stand in for the dairy department manager during the summer of 2024. Beginning in 2025 Josh joined the Newmanstown Volunteer Fire Company.
